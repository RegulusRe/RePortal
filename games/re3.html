Resident Evil 3 Remake

Release Date: April 3, 2020

Resident Evil 3 Remake brings the harrowing escape of Jill Valentine to life with modern graphics and gameplay innovations. Set in Raccoon City, players experience the terrifying evacuation of the city as it becomes ground zero for a bioweapon apocalypse, all while being hunted by the relentless bio-engineered monster, Nemesis.

This remake captures the tension and horror of the original while delivering enhanced combat mechanics, stunning environments, and cinematic storytelling. Players must navigate through infected streets, solve environmental puzzles, and manage limited resources while evading or confronting the iconic and terrifying Nemesis. RE3 Remake successfully delivers intense survival horror action.

Genre

Survival Horror

Platforms

PC, PS4, Xbox One

Developer

Capcom

Rating
