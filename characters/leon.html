Leon S. Kennedy

Role: Protagonist / Police Officer

Leon S. Kennedy is one of the most iconic characters in the Resident Evil franchise. A rookie police officer who arrives at Raccoon City on his first day of work, only to find himself thrust into a nightmare of biological horror and survival. Throughout multiple games, Leon has evolved from a young, inexperienced cop into a hardened veteran of countless bioweapon encounters.

Known for his determination, combat prowess, and unwavering resolve, Leon has faced countless horrors including the T-virus, Las Plagas parasites, and numerous bioweapon creatures. His story spans multiple timelines and locations, making him a central figure in the Resident Evil universe. Despite the traumatic experiences, Leon continues to fight against bioterrorism threats.

First Appearance

Resident Evil 2 (1998)

Specialties

Combat, Weaponry, Investigation

Status

Active B.S.A.A. Agent

Notable Games

RE2, RE4, RE6, RE Vendetta
