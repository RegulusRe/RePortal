Ada Wong

Role: Enigmatic Spy / Bioweapon Analyst

Ada Wong is a mysterious and highly skilled spy whose true allegiances remain ambiguous throughout her appearances in the Resident Evil series. Possessing exceptional espionage training, technological expertise, and combat proficiency, Ada operates in the shadows of bioweapon conspiracies, often working multiple angles to achieve her enigmatic goals.

A master of deception and infiltration, Ada has involved herself in major bioweapon incidents across multiple locations. Her intelligence work and technical skills make her invaluable to various factions, though her true loyalties remain unclear. Ada represents the gray area between protagonist and antagonist, surviving encounters with bioweapons and powerful organizations through cunning and precision. Her complex motivations and survival instincts have made her one of the series' most intriguing characters.

First Appearance

Resident Evil 4 (2005)

Specialties

Espionage, Hacking, Combat

Status

Independent Operative

Notable Games

RE4, RE5, RE6
