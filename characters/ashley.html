Ashley Graham

Role: U.S. President's Daughter / Survivor

Ashley Graham is the daughter of the U.S. President who becomes the central focus of Leon Kennedy's rescue mission. Kidnapped and forced into a dangerous conspiracy involving bioweapon experiments and cult activities, Ashley must survive against horrifying odds while being hunted by sinister forces.

Though initially appearing as a damsel in distress, Ashley proves herself to be resourceful and brave, learning to adapt to extreme survival situations. Her journey through bioweapon-infested locations and encounters with cultist operatives demonstrates her resilience. Ashley's experience transforms her from a sheltered political figure into someone who understands the true dangers lurking in the world of bioterrorism.
