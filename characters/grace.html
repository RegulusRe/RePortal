Grace Ashcroft

Role: Operative / Strategic Analyst

Grace Ashcroft is a tactical operative and strategic analyst who brings intelligence and precision to bioweapon containment operations. With her background in advanced surveillance and tactical planning, Grace has become instrumental in coordinating responses to bioterrorism threats across multiple regions.

As a member of elite anti-bioterrorism units, Grace specializes in threat assessment, team coordination, and tactical decision-making under pressure. Her analytical mind and combat training make her an invaluable asset in the fight against biological threats. Grace represents a new generation of agents trained to combat increasingly sophisticated bioweapon incidents in the modern era.
