Nemesis

Type: Bio-Organic Weapon (B.O.W.) / Hunter Class

Nemesis is one of the most iconic and terrifying bio-engineered creatures in the Resident Evil universe. Created through genetic engineering and equipped with advanced combat capabilities, Nemesis was designed as a Hunter-class B.O.W. (Bio-Organic Weapon) programmed for relentless pursuit and elimination of targets.

Standing as a towering, grotesque amalgamation of biological enhancements and combat technology, Nemesis represents the apex of weaponized bioengineering gone horribly wrong. This nearly unstoppable force hunts its prey with methodical precision and overwhelming strength. Nemesis symbolizes the ultimate threat posed by rogue bioweapon programs and serves as a harbinger of bioterrorism's devastating potential on a global scale.

First Appearance

Resident Evil 3 (1999)

Classification

Hunter-Class B.O.W.

Status

Active Threat

Notable Games

RE3, RE3 Remake
